---
title: Energy Stats
description: Today's solar, consumption, import and export figures.
---

The Energy Stats section shows today's cumulative energy figures, pulled directly from your inverter's registers.

## Figures shown

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Solar (Today) | Total energy generated by your solar panels today |
| Home (Today) | Total energy consumed by your home today |
| Import (Today) | Total energy imported from the grid today |
| Export (Today) | Total energy exported to the grid today |

Values are shown in kWh and reset at midnight.

## Battery card

Above the energy stats, a battery card shows the current state of the selected device including:

- State of charge (%)
- Current charge or discharge power
- Estimated time to full or empty
- Total capacity and usable capacity

Swipe the battery card to see stats for other devices in your system.
