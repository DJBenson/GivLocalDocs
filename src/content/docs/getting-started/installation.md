## Android

GivLocal is available now on Google Play.

[**Download on Google Play**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.thomserve.givlocal)

## iOS & iPadOS

An App Store release is coming soon. GivLocal runs on iPhone and iPad.

## Requirements

- Android 8.0 or later, or iOS 16 or later
- Connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your GivEnergy inverter or gateway (for local access)
